About About Discover Galway

Our Mission:

To help visitors and locals easily navigate and experience the best of Galway City through a simple, all-in-one solution.

The Problem:

  • Finding up-to-date information on attractions
  • Discovering local events during their stay
  • Locating nearby food & drink options
  • Navigating public transportation
  • Accessing emergency information

Our Solution:

A comprehensive city guide that works offline, providing everything you need to enjoy Galway in one convenient app.
